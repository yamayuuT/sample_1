蕎麦(soba)

内容物(contents)

そば(soba)
そばつゆ(soba sauce)
刻みねぎ(shredded green onion)
わさび(wasabi: Wasabia japonica)

　日本そば(蕎麦)は、ソバの実を使った蕎麦粉を加工した麺類です。日本全国で食べられていますが、全国製麺協同組合連合会では、そば粉30%以上、小麦粉70%以下の割合で混合した原料を用いたものを日本そばと呼んでいます。
好みに合わせて色々な食べ方を楽しめるのが日本そばの魅力です。代表的なものが、一口ずつつゆにつけて食べる「もりそば」「ざるそば」と、丼に温かいつゆをたっぷりかける「かけそば」です。また、10月の新そばの季節にはとくに香り高いそばが味わえます。

Japanese buckwheat noodles (soba) are noodles made from buckwheat flour processed from buckwheat seeds. It is eaten throughout Japan, but the National Federation of Noodle-Making Cooperative Associations calls it Japanese buckwheat noodles when the ingredients are a mixture of more than 30% buckwheat flour and less than 70% wheat flour.
The appeal of Japanese soba is that it can be enjoyed in a variety of ways according to taste. Typical examples are morisoba and zarusoba, which are eaten one bite at a time dipped in tsuyu, and kakesoba, which is served over a bowl of rice with plenty of warm tsuyu. In October, when the new soba season is in full bloom, the soba is especially aromatic.
